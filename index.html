Professional rehearsal and recording facility

Dockside Studios is a professional rehearsal and recording facility, situated in central Bristol and run by professional musicians for musicians. It is equipped with purpose built rooms for recording and rehearsing. There is ample free parking by the main entrance. Our rates are £10 per hour for rehearsal rooms and £20 per hour for recording (£5 per hour from 12 noon to 4pm Weekdays for rehearsals, excluding Bank Holidays).

Rehearsal Rooms

Our practice rooms are fitted with 900watt full range systems, Mackie SRM450 active speakers and Soundcraft Mixing desks as standard.

Recording Studio

We have a fully equipped recording setup built around Protools and a Fostex E16 ½" reel to reel, offering upwards of 60 tracks in total. Other equipment includes a 72ch mackie 8bus desk, and outboard processors by Focusrite, Drawmer, Alesis, Lexicon, Roland, and Digitec. All recording work is carried out by our own in-house engineers.

PA Hire

Dockside Studios has several P.A. packages available, ranging from a vocal P.A. (powered mixer, speakers, mics, leads and stands) to a full-range 2K system with engineer. P.A. packages can be tailored to suit individual requirements so contact the Studios for prices.
