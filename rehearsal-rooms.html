Rehearsal rooms for band practices

Here at Dockside we believe that the most important aspect of a rehearsal room is the quality of the P.A. system. With this in mind, all our practice rooms are fitted with 900watt full range systems capable of handling all modern musical equipment. This includes Mackie SRM450 active speakers and Soundcraft Mixing desks as standard. Our rates are £10 per hour, booked by the hour. Call us on 0117 934 9994 to book.

Rehearsal Rooms

We charge £10 per hour with a special rate of £5 per hour from 12 noon to 4pm Mon-Fri for rehearsals (excluding Bank Holidays).


Backline is available to hire (drums, amps etc) for £5 per item per session.


Storage is also available from £5 per week


Tea, coffee and water are provided free of charge and a kitchen area with microwave is also available.


You'll love our friendly relaxed atmosphere and helpful support crew. Dockside Studios is owned and operated by Professional Musicians, so you are dealing with people that understand and know your requirements. The owners, Ellie and Steve, have been known to help out musically when required.
